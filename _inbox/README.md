# Inbox

Drop anything here. Seriously, anything:

- Pasted transcript
- Meeting notes
- Quick observation ("Sarah seemed nervous about timeline")
- Voice memo transcription
- Screenshot of a whiteboard
- FigJam export
- Half-formed thought
- Link to something interesting

**No naming conventions. No formatting required. Just drop it.**

---

## What happens next

1. Type `process inbox` in Cursor
2. AI reads everything here, extracts the good stuff
3. Proposals appear in `_pending/REVIEW.md`
4. You review and approve when you have 5 minutes
5. Processed files move to `_processed/`

That's it. You drop, AI sorts, you approve.
